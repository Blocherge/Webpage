ABOUT ME

I'm a first year student at CU Boulder. I'm looking to get my degree in CS with a minor in creative technology and design. I have a few hobbies, one of which you can guess by the profile picture is working on classic cars. I also enjoy gaming, classic rock, and working on computers.

On more of a job perspective, I have done CS work since around 6th grade when I started working with arduino boards on bots. I learned to program those bots so they could follow a color based map, recognize start conditions, and even sumo wrestle another bot. When I hit middle school I picked up javascript using an online course that taught me the basics, and have continued from there. Admittedly though, coding is not what started my passion for CS. When I was young the only thing I wanted to do was take apart old mechanical systems and see what made them tick. I started with old radios and tube TV's and then wanted to learn how code made those systems really spark.
