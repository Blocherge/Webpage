Swerve_Sim

Swerve_Sim is a java/lwjgl based swerve drive simulator for FRC teams to get practice driving the robots earlier in the season to give them a competitive chance being able to practice more. Currently it is setup with last years Crescendo game, and doesn't really have either the graphics or the movement/customization that I would like it to. I've been looking at remaking the project in Godot since it is a more built engine and easier to use.
